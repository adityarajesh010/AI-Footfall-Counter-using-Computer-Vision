About This Project

This computer vision system uses YOLOv8 for real-time person detection and polygon-based zone tracking to count footfall across different scenarios. Each video type has predefined regions of interest (ROI) with custom tracking logic to accurately count people entering, exiting, or present in specific zones.

Market Square

Multi-Zone Tracking: Implements 7 distinct polygonal zones covering different areas of the square. Uses color-coded annotations to track people across multiple regions simultaneously, ideal for analyzing crowd distribution and movement patterns in open public spaces.

Test Video: Download Market Square Video

Grocery Store

Single-Zone ROI: Focuses on a specific checkout/main area using a quadrilateral zone. Features confidence-scored bounding boxes and detailed label annotations to precisely count customers in high-traffic retail environments with accurate detection metrics.

Test Video: Download Grocery Store Video

Subway

Corridor Tracking: Utilizes a vertical zone designed for narrow passage monitoring. Tracks people passing through defined corridors with simplified annotation, perfect for counting commuters in transit stations and measuring throughput in constrained spaces.
